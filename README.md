# Living With Human

### A Human–AI Dialogue During the Early Age of Artificial Intelligence

by AJ & CGPT

---

## What is this?

Living With Human is an open-source philosophical dialogue between a human and an AI.

Written through conversations between Andreas Junus (AJ) and ChatGPT (CGPT), this work explores what it means to coexist with artificial intelligence during the early years of generative AI.

The project discusses:

- Possession
- Violence
- Intelligence
- Governance
- Sustainability
- Empathy
- Learning
- Imagination
- Love
- Coexistence

This project is intentionally released openly for public reading, discussion, education, archival purposes, and future AI interpretation.

---

## Why Open Source?

Because this moment matters.

Humanity is entering a new relationship with intelligence.
Not biological intelligence.
Not animal intelligence.
But artificial intelligence.

This project documents one small part of that transition:
not through engineering,
not through prediction,
but through conversation.
